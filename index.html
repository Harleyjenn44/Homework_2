About Me

Hello! This is all about me! My name is Ashton and I was born and raised in Kansas City. I graduated from Sumner Academy of the Arts and Sciences. I originally wanted to become a pharmacist when I graduated from high school but found out that it wasn't the career for me. So here I am. I'm on my way to becoming a software engineer. I chose this as a career because my passion is volunteering for different non-profits. My favorite non-profit is To Write Love On Her Arms. They offer a lot a support for people struggling with addiction or mental health.

For fun, I enjoy spending time with my friends and animals. I have two amazing best friends and one amazing boyfriend. My mom and they are my biggest supporters for this career.

With this career, I hope to be able to help my community and be able to raise a family comfortably. I love computers and I'm a really quick learner. That is why I think I'll do great in this field and make a great career for me.
